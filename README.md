## Process:

## Gender-Based Insights
•	The shopping distribution indicates that one gender (typically females) contributes a higher number of transactions. 
•	More products were sold to females, suggesting higher purchase frequency. 
•	However, male customers may generate higher revenue per transaction, indicating a tendency toward higher-value purchases.
## Age-Based Insights
•	The majority of shopping activity comes from the 18–35 age group, making them the most active customers. 
•	The 26–35 age category tends to purchase the highest number of products. 
•	The 36–50 age group often generates the highest revenue due to stronger purchasing power.
Product Category Insights
•	Certain categories like Fashion and Electronics dominate overall sales. 
•	Gender influence: 
•	Males prefer electronics and gadgets 
•	Age influence: 
•	Younger customers → trendy and affordable items 
•	Older customers → premium and utility-based products.
## Overall Insight
Customer purchasing behavior is strongly influenced by gender, age, product category, and payment method. By leveraging these insights, the company can increase sales, improve customer satisfaction, and optimize business strategies.

## Final Conclusion
The analysis shows that customer demographics (gender & age), product categories, and payment methods strongly influence purchasing behavior. By leveraging these insights, the company can increase sales, improve customer targeting, and enhance overall profitability.
